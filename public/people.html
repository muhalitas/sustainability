People

The S3-Lab team at Istanbul Sehir University involves a diverse group of dedicated academicians, students, business professionals, and international collaborators.

Dr. Murat Küçükvar

Dr. Küçükvar is a founding member and co-director at the Sustainable Systems & Solutions (S3-Lab). He also serves as the Chairman and Assistant Professor of the Department of Industrial Engineering at Istanbul Sehir University, Turkey. He holds the positions of International Programs and Erasmus Coordinator, Member of the Board of Directors at Graduate School of Engineering and Applied Sciences, and Member of Quality Commission at Sehir. At S3-Lab, he develops decision making tools and methods for sustainable systems analysis.

Research Expertise: : Industrial Sustainability, Sustainable and Smart Cities, Energy-Water-Climate Nexus, Sustainable Production and Consumption, Sustainable Operations and Supply Chain Management, Multi Region Input-Output Analysis, Life Cycle Sustainability Assessment, and Multi Criteria Decision Analysis.

Email: muratkucukvar@sehir.edu.tr

Phone: +90 444 4 034 / 9256

Dr. Nuri Cihat Onat

Dr. Onat is the Co-founder and Co-director of S3-Lab. He serves as an Assistant Professor at the Department of Industrial Engineering at Istanbul Sehir University (SEHIR). He is also a senior sustainability fellow and an online instructor at the School of Sustainability at the Arizona State University. Prior to joining the SEHIR, he worked as a Walton Postdoctoral Research Fellow at the Arizona State University as part of the Walton Sustainability Fellowship Program. At the S3-Lab, his primary responsibilities include developing system-based sustainability solutions and sustainability impact assessments for complex sustainability problems at various scales.

Research Expertise: System Dynamics, Economic Input-Output Analysis, Life Cycle Sustainability Assessment, and Decision-Making. Applications of his work cover Transportation, Built Environment, Energy, and Manufacturing Systems.

Email: nurionat@sehir.edu.tr

Phone: +90 444 4 034 / 9958
