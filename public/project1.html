Energy Systems

Energy systems research plays a central role in S3-Lab’s research efforts as it relates to rest of the thematic research areas (Manufacturing, transportation, the built environment). The need for reaching low-cost energy, securing national energy supply, reducing environmental impacts, and contribution of energy expenses to national trade deficit are some of the most important challenges. In this regard, energy systems research focuses on environmental, economic, social, technological, and strategic assessment of emerging and clean energy technologies and anticipated problems associated with energy security, systems, and infrastructure.
