Sustainable Transportation

While there is need for improving access to goods and services to support economic and social development, at the same time, the adverse environmental, social and economic impacts of exponentially growing transportation sector must be minimized. With this motivation, S3-Lab investigates impacts of wide range of alternative transportation modes, options, and technologies to develop effective policies and decision support tools.
