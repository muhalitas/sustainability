Sustainable Built Environment

The built environment refers to man-made surroundings including buildings, roads, bridges, and other infrastructure providing the setting for human activity. The built environment has a wide range of impacts to societies, the environment, and economies. Therefore, planning and management in the built environment is a crucial topic to achieve sustainable development goals. In the field of built environment, S3-Lab’s focus ranges from green buildings, asphalt, construction materials, bridges to macro-level planning challenges.
