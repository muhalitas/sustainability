Water Management and Assessment

Water is an essential and valuable resource for human, communities, cities, nations, and globe. Rapid Urbanization, increasing population, and global warming increased the concerns related to preservation and management of fresh water resources. S3-Lab focuses on macro-level water management issues, water footprint analysis of emerging technologies, and conflicts related to water usage and rights.
