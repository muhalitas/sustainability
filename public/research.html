Research

S3-Lab is a unique research laboratory based at the Istanbul Sehir University, Turkey. Our research group aims to help sustainable development, project implementation, and community outreach by addressing sustainability challenges in both local and global scale. The work in S3-Lab is motivated by the principles of understanding and addressing the opportunities and risks/challenges towards sustainable development goals.

Considering that the sustainability problems are interdisciplinary by its nature, achieving sustainable solutions requires collaborative efforts beyond the boundaries of traditional disciplines. Therefore, our research efforts focus on establishing integrated system analyses and frameworks in which a wide range of decision-making tools and disciplines are harmonized and applied for a wide range of sustainability challenges. The applications of our research encompass the fields of energy, transportation, manufacturing, and the built environment.
